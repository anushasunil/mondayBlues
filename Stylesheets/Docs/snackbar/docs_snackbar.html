Snackbar

A Snackbar is a message used to give users feedback after taking an action. They are placed at the bottom of the screen and they vanish after few seconds.

Baseline

Failed to send message. Retry in 5 seconds

RETRY 

<div class="snackbars baseline display-align-center">
    <p class="message">
        Failed to send message.
        <span class="suggestion">
            Retry in 5 seconds
        </span>
    </p>
    <div class="actions-baseline display-align-center">
        <span class="action-btn clickable-image">RETRY</span>
        <img src="/Assets/icons/close_white.svg" alt="close" class="dismiss clickable-image">
    </div>
</div>
